Jenny Yzaguirre

Project Manager

Jenny Yzaguirre is a seasoned Construction Project Manager specializing in high-end residential construction, guiding projects seamlessly from pre-construction through final closeout. With a meticulous eye for detail, she expertly coordinates trades, manages complex schedules and budgets, and ensures every element of construction meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Jenny’s expertise spans subcontractor management, cost control, and on-site operations, working in close collaboration with architects, engineers, inspectors, and discerning homeowners to maintain momentum and protect the integrity of each project. Her hands-on leadership and proactive problem-solving allow her to anticipate field conditions, resolve challenges with precision, and uphold exceptional execution throughout every phase of construction.

Known for her polished communication style, organized leadership, and dynamic presence on site, Jenny fosters accountability while cultivating a positive, professional team environment. She excels in overseeing timelines, managing submittals and RFIs, tracking change orders, and delivering refined, thoughtfully executed residences that consistently exceed client expectations.
